(The Quiz to end all Quizzes.)

The Great War (World War One) was a large-scale war fought between the world's most influential powers at the time (concentrated in Europe). Some nations in the war were the British Empire, The German Empire, The Republic of France, The Russian Empire, The Austro-Hungarian Empire, and the Ottoman Empire. The war quickly turned from a traditionally fought fight into a war of attrition where neither side could advance and take the other's positions due to the development of trench warfare.
